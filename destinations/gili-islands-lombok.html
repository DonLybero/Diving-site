Gili Islands & Lombok

Indonesia · Southeast Asia — Reliable year-round turtles over easy reefs, ideal for beginners

Photo: Tilonaut (CC BY 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Gili Islands & Lombok is at its best May-Sep, with May, Jun, Jul, Aug and Sep rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Lombok Strait drift common; training sites gentle. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 2 reefs, 2 pinnacles, 1 wall, 1 wreck, 1 drift dive and 1 muck dive — ranging from Meno Wall (5-20m) for beginner divers up to Deep Turbo (18-32m), advanced territory.
