Bunaken National Marine Park

Indonesia · Southeast Asia — Sheer Sulawesi walls teeming with turtles and reef fish, superb visibility

Photo: kikofjl (CC BY 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Bunaken National Marine Park is at its best Mar-Oct, with Apr, May, Jun, Jul, Aug and Sep rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 28–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Wall drift, usually mild, occasionally strong. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 7 walls and 1 reef — ranging from Lekuan Walls (I-III) (5-35m+) for beginner divers up to Tanjung Kopi (8-28m), advanced territory.
