Moorea

French Polynesia · South Pacific — Lemon-shark dives and seasonal humpback whale encounters

Photo: Charles J. Sharp (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Moorea is at its best Jul-Oct, with Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 26–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Minimal current, some surge; beginner-friendly. Divers here work 7 recognised sites — 4 reefs, 1 shark dive, 1 pass and 1 drift dive — ranging from Opunohu Canyons (10-20m) for beginner divers up to The Rose Garden (35-45m), advanced territory.
