Poor Knights Islands

New Zealand · South Pacific — Cousteau-praised reserve - arches, caves, subtropical fish schools

Photo: Shaun Lee (CC BY 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Poor Knights Islands is at its best Jan-Apr (NZ summer/autumn), with Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr and Dec rating as peak season. Expect saltwater of 15–22°C (suit: 7mm wetsuit), visibility up to ~28m, and medium currents — Arches/headlands can run strong; most sites moderate. Divers here work 7 recognised sites — 4 archs, 1 cave, 1 pinnacle and 1 reef — ranging from Rikoriko Cave (5-20m) for beginner divers up to Landing Bay Pinnacle (6-45m), advanced territory.
