Apo Island & Dumaguete

Philippines · Southeast Asia — Sea turtles on Apo's reefs plus world-class muck diving at Dauin

Photo: jackylim (CC BY-SA 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Apo Island & Dumaguete is at its best Mar-Jun, with Mar, Apr and May rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Apo points strong drift; Dauin muck calm. Divers here work 10 recognised sites — 4 muck dives, 3 reefs, 2 drift dives and 1 wall — ranging from Rock Point West (5-20m) for beginner divers up to Cogon Point (8-30m), advanced territory.
