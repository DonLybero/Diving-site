Great Barrier Reef

Australia · Coral Sea / Australia — World's largest reef; dwarf minke whales Jun-Jul; coral spawning Nov.

Photo: Pauline Walsh Jacobson (CC BY 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Great Barrier Reef is at its best Jun-Oct, with May, Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 23–30°C (suit: 3mm summer / 5mm winter), visibility up to ~70m, and medium currents — Cod Hole gentle; strong drift only at Osprey/Coral Sea. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 3 reefs, 3 pinnacles, 2 walls and 1 wreck — ranging from Cod Hole (10-25m) for beginner divers up to North Horn (10-40m), advanced territory.
