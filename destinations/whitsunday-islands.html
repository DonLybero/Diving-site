Whitsunday Islands

Australia · Coral Sea / Great Barrier Reef — Heart Reef and Hardy Reef coral gardens with winter whale encounters

Photo: O.V.E.R.V.I.E.W. (CC BY 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Whitsunday Islands is at its best Jun-Oct (best viz, humpbacks Jun-Sep), with Jun, Jul, Aug and Sep rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 23–29°C (suit: 3mm summer, 5mm winter), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Large tidal range drives light-to-strong currents. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 4 reefs, 2 bommies, 1 pinnacle and 1 wall — ranging from Manta Ray Bay (3-18m) for beginner divers up to The Hangover (10-25m), intermediate territory.
