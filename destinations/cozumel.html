Cozumel

Mexico · Caribbean — Effortless high-viz drift dives along vibrant Mesoamerican reef walls

Photo: Serge Melki from Indianapolis, USA (CC BY 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Cozumel is at its best May-Aug (warm, calm); year-round diving, with Apr, May, Jun and Jul rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 26–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and strong currents — Yucatan Current defines drift diving here. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 3 drift dives, 2 walls, 1 cavern, 1 wreck and 1 reef — ranging from Palancar Reef (5-30m) for beginner divers up to Punta Sur (Devil's Throat) (24-40m), advanced territory.
