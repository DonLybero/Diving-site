French Polynesia

French Polynesia · South Pacific — Atoll passes with grey-reef shark walls and big-fish drifts

French Polynesia is at its best Jun-Oct, with Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 26–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~35m, and strong currents — Tiputa/Fakarava pass currents can be mask-ripping. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 5 pass drifts, 2 reefs and 2 shark dives — ranging from Anau (Bora Bora) (10-20m) for beginner divers up to Avatoru Pass (Rangiroa) (15-30m), advanced territory.
