Sea of Cortez

Mexico · Gulf of California / Pacific — Los Islotes sea lions, mobula swarms and La Paz whale sharks

Photo: Marty Purdy (CC BY 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Sea of Cortez is at its best Aug-Nov (warm, clear), Oct-Apr whale sharks, with Sep, Oct and Nov rating as peak season. Expect subtropical water of 20–30°C (suit: 5-7mm winter, 3mm summer), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Signature sites mild-moderate; seamounts stronger. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 4 reefs, 3 wrecks and 1 seamount — ranging from Los Islotes (5-18m) for beginner divers up to El Bajo (18-40m), advanced territory.
