Lundy Island

United Kingdom · North Atlantic / Europe — UK's first marine reserve - playful grey seal encounters

Photo: Philip Halling (CC BY-SA 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Lundy Island is at its best Jun-Sep, with Jun, Jul and Aug rating as peak season. Expect saltwater of 8–16°C (suit: Drysuit recommended (cold UK water)), visibility up to ~12m, and medium currents — Tidal Bristol Channel; seal coves sheltered. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 3 reefs, 3 wrecks, 1 pinnacle and 1 wall — ranging from Gannets' Bay (5-15m) for beginner divers up to Battery Point (10-25m), advanced territory.
