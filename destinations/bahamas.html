Bahamas

Bahamas · Caribbean — World's premier shark diving - hammerheads, tigers, and whitetips

Photo: James St. John (CC BY 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Bahamas is at its best Nov-May (sharks peak in winter), with Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr, May, Nov and Dec rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 24–30°C (suit: 5mm in winter, 3mm summer), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Gulf Stream drift at Bimini; Tiger Beach variable. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 2 shark dives, 2 blue holes, 2 wrecks and 2 drift dives — ranging from Tiger Beach (6-12m) for intermediate divers up to Current Cut (10-20m), advanced territory.
