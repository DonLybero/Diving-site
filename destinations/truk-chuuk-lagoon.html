Truk (Chuuk) Lagoon

Micronesia (FSM) · Micronesia / Pacific — World's premier WWII wreck dive; 50+ ships in a calm lagoon.

Photo: Taken by User:Legis at en.wikipedia (CC0) via Wikimedia Commons

Truk (Chuuk) Lagoon is at its best Dec-Apr, with Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr and Dec rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 28–30°C (suit: 3mm (5mm for deep repeats)), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Sheltered lagoon, minimal current on wrecks. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 8 wrecks — ranging from Betty Bomber (15-18m) for beginner divers up to San Francisco Maru (50-63m), technical-diving territory.

What to pack here

Water sits around 29°C here, so a 3 mm wetsuit is the right call. A 5 mm is worth packing for winter or deeper, repeat dives.
