Fernando de Noronha

Brazil · Atlantic Ocean / Brazil — Huge spinner dolphin pods with sharks, turtles and 30m+ visibility

Photo: Roberta monte (CC BY-SA 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Fernando de Noronha is at its best Aug-Dec (dry season, best viz), with Aug, Sep, Oct and Nov rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 26–28°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and medium currents — Leeward mild; windward and Dec-Mar currents stronger. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 4 reefs, 2 caverns, 1 wreck and 1 drift dive — ranging from Buraco do Inferno (8-20m) for beginner divers up to Corveta V17 Ipiranga (58-62m), technical-diving territory.
