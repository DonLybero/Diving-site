Malpelo Island

Colombia · Eastern Pacific / Colombia — Walls of 200-plus schooling scalloped hammerheads, silky shark hordes and the rare smalltooth sand tiger on a remote volcanic seamount 500km offshore.

Photo: Jdmacarenoq (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Malpelo Island is at its best Dec-Mar (peak hammerhead schools), with Jan, Feb, Mar, Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Dec rating as peak season. Expect tropical/subtropical water of 24–28°C (suit: 5mm (7mm or hooded for Dec-May thermoclines down to ~14C)), visibility up to ~30m, and strong currents — Fierce; reef hooks and drift skills essential. Divers here work 7 recognised sites — 3 pinnacles, 1 cleaning station, 1 seamount, 1 wall and 1 reef slope — ranging from La Nevera (The Fridge) (15-40m) for advanced divers up to El Altar de la Virgen (15-35m), advanced territory.

Recognised dive sites (7)

Depths are typical published ranges — always confirm with your operator.

SiteTypeDepthLevelWhy it’s known
La Nevera (The Fridge)Cleaning station15-40madvancedSignature cleaning station on the northeast side where scalloped hammerheads and Galapagos sharks queue among reef fish; cold upwelling gives it its name.
El Bajo del Monstruo (Monster Seamount)Seamount20-45madvancedOffshore pinnacle famous for hundreds of hammerheads and the elusive smalltooth sand tiger that rises to ~21m in the cold season; divable only in good conditions.
La GringaPinnacle24-40madvancedExposed monolith on the south side pierced by a swim-through cave between 24-40m; hammerheads, Galapagos and silky sharks, tuna and eagle rays in fierce current.
El FreezerWall15-40madvancedVolcanic wall partly clad in hard coral, named for the cold water on this flank; walls of hammerheads and passing pelagics dropping into the blue.
Los Tres MosqueterosPinnacle15-35madvancedTrio of rocks off the north coast delivering classic pinnacle diving with silky and Galapagos sharks plus schooling tuna.
SaharaReef slope15-30madvancedSouthern site where silky sharks congregate in large numbers over a current-swept slope.
El Altar de la VirgenPinnacle15-35madvancedColorful hard-coral pinnacle reliable for hammerhead sightings and cruising eagle rays.
