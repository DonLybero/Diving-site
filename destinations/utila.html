Utila

Honduras · Caribbean — Budget dive-cert hub with year-round whale shark chances

Photo: henrya (CC0) via Wikimedia Commons

Utila is at its best Feb-Apr & Oct-Dec (whale sharks), with Feb, Mar and Apr rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–30°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Calm south-coast sites, low current. Divers here work 6 recognised sites — 2 walls, 1 pinnacle, 1 wreck, 1 reef and 1 cavern — ranging from Ted's Point (5-20m) for beginner divers up to Halliburton Wreck (18-30m), advanced territory.
