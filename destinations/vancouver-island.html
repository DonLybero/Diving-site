Vancouver Island

Canada · Pacific Northwest — Cold-water Pacific - wolf eels, giant octopus, sea lions

Photo: Earth Science and Remote Sensing Unit, Lyndon B. Johnson Space Center (Public domain) via Wikimedia Commons

Vancouver Island is at its best Oct-Mar (best viz), with Jan, Feb, Oct, Nov and Dec rating as peak season. Expect saltwater of 7–16°C (suit: Drysuit only (cold year-round)), visibility up to ~25m, and strong currents — Tidal rapids; current-timed sites. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 3 wrecks, 2 walls, 2 drift dives, 1 reef and 1 shore dive — ranging from Ogden Point Breakwater (5-18m) for beginner divers up to Nakwakto Rapids (Turret Rock) (10-22m), advanced territory.
