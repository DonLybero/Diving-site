Fiji

Fiji · South Pacific — Soft-coral capital with Beqa Lagoon bull-shark dives

Fiji is at its best Jul-Oct, with Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 25–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and strong currents — Current-driven drift dives feed the soft corals. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 2 walls, 2 reefs, 2 pass drifts, 1 shark dive, 1 drift dive and 1 pinnacle — ranging from Rainbow Reef (5-30m) for beginner divers up to Purple Wall (10-30m), intermediate territory.
