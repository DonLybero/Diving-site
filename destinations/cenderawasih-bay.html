Cenderawasih Bay

Indonesia · Coral Triangle / West Papua (Indonesia) — The only place on Earth to scuba year-round with resident whale sharks feeding under fishermen's bagan platforms, plus coral-clad WWII wrecks in glassy, currentless water.

Photo: Bird's Head Seascape / Jones/Shimlock / Secret Sea Visions (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Cenderawasih Bay is at its best Jun-Oct (whale sharks year-round; best liveaboard season), with Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–30°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~28m, and low currents — calm, almost currentless. Divers here work 7 recognised sites — 3 wrecks, 1 whale shark platforms, 1 soft-coral reef, 1 wall / drop-off and 1 coral reef — ranging from Kwatisore Bagans (3-12m) for beginner divers up to P-40 Tomahawk Plane Wreck (20-30m), intermediate territory.

Recognised dive sites (7)

Depths are typical published ranges — always confirm with your operator.

SiteTypeDepthLevelWhy it’s known
Kwatisore Baganswhale shark platforms3-12mbeginnerThe signature dive: divers descend beside the anchored fishing platforms where resident whale sharks hang vertically to suck baitfish from the nets, giving unhurried, close scuba encounters in warm, currentless water.
Tanjung Mangguarsoft-coral reef5-30mbeginnerOne of the bay's prettiest reefs, blanketed in soft coral and swarmed by barracuda, trevally, fusiliers and surgeonfish along an easy sloping wall.
Shinwa Maruwreck16-34mintermediateA 120m Japanese WWII cargo ship lying on its port side, largely intact and now a coral-encrusted reef draped in hard corals and hosting dense fish life.
Cross Wreckwreck12-30mbeginnerA shallow, easy Japanese wartime wreck coated in hard coral and marked by a memorial cross, ideal for relaxed exploration and macro life.
Tridacna Atollwall / drop-off5-35mbeginnerDeep drop-offs and fringing reefs carpeted in soft corals, sea whips, giant clams and massive barrel and elephant-ear sponges.
P-40 Tomahawk Plane Wreckwreck20-30mintermediateA remarkably preserved WWII fighter aircraft resting upright with instrumentation and pilot's seat still in place, sitting quietly on the calm bay floor.
Purup Island Reefscoral reef5-25mbeginnerSheltered fringing reefs rich in West Papua's endemic reef fish, healthy hard corals and abundant macro subjects in flat, easy conditions.
