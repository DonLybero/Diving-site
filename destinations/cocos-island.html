Cocos Island

Costa Rica · Eastern Pacific — Hammerhead-school mecca; remote 36h crossing, strong currents/thermoclines.

Photo: Robert Webster (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Cocos Island is at its best Jun-Nov (hammerheads) / Dec-May (visibility), with Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical-temperate water of 25–29°C (suit: 5-7mm + hood), visibility up to ~32m, and strong currents — Strong wet-season currents, advanced-only pelagic diving. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 3 pinnacles, 2 seamounts, 2 reefs and 1 shark dive — ranging from Manuelita (10-25m) for intermediate divers up to Submerged Rock (12-30m), advanced territory.

What to pack here

Water sits around 27°C here, so a 5 mm wetsuit is the right call. A 7 mm is worth packing for winter or deeper, repeat dives.
