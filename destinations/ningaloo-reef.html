Ningaloo Reef

Australia · Indian Ocean / Western Australia — Whale shark snorkeling on the reef plus year-round mantas and turtles

Photo: W. Bulach (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Ningaloo Reef is at its best Mar-Jul (whale sharks), with Apr, May, Jun and Jul rating as peak season. Expect tropical/subtropical water of 22–29°C (suit: 3mm, 5mm winter), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Lagoon gentle; tidal passes need slack-tide planning. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 6 reefs and 2 shore dives — ranging from Blizzard Ridge (8-16m) for beginner divers up to Helga's Tunnels (8-16m), intermediate territory.
