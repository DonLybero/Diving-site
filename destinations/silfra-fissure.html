Silfra Fissure

Iceland · North Atlantic / Europe — Crystal 100m+ viz freshwater dive between tectonic plates

Photo: Sergei Gussev (CC BY 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Silfra Fissure is at its best Jun-Aug, with Jun, Jul and Aug rating as peak season. Expect freshwater of 2–4°C (suit: Drysuit only (near-freezing year-round)), visibility up to ~100m, and low currents — Gentle drift current only. Divers here work 5 recognised sites — 5 fissures — ranging from Silfra Big Crack (7-18m) for beginner divers up to Davíðsgjá (10-21m), intermediate territory.
