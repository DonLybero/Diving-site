Maldives

Maldives · Indian Ocean — Monsoon-driven channel drifts; Hanifaru manta aggregation Jul-Oct.

Photo: Tiennegolf (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons
🏆 Best months: Jan, Feb, Mar, Apr, Jul, Aug, Sep, Oct, Dec  ·  Recommended window: Dec-Apr (clear water) / May-Nov (mantas & whale sharks)
Water typetropical
Difficultybeginner-intermediate
Accessliveaboard/resort
Wetsuit3mm
Current strengthMedium
Tidal channel currents, manageable drift; strong at kandus
Currents detailMonsoon-driven channel (kandu) drift; NE monsoon (Dec-Apr) dive east side for viz/pelagics, mantas west; SW monsoon (May-Nov) dive west side, mantas/whale sharks east; strongest at full/new moon
