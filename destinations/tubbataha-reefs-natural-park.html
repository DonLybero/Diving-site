Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park

Philippines · Southeast Asia — UNESCO atoll with sharks, mantas and pristine walls, liveaboard-only short season

Photo: Gregg Yan (CC BY-SA 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park is at its best Apr-Jun, with Apr and May rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 28–30°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and strong currents — Persistent currents; all dives drift with live boat. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 3 reefs, 2 walls, 2 wrecks and 1 drift dive — ranging from Shark Airport (8-25m) for intermediate divers up to Black Rock (10-30m), advanced territory.
