Komodo National Park

Indonesia · Coral Triangle / SE Asia — Strong tidal drifts; warm north vs cold-upwelling south; mantas year-round by zone.

Photo: Nhobgood (CC BY-SA 3.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Komodo National Park is at its best Apr-Oct (May best) / Dec-Feb (south mantas), with Apr, May, Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–29°C (suit: 5mm), visibility up to ~30m, and strong currents — Ripping tidal currents, reef hooks standard. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 3 drift dives, 3 reefs, 2 pinnacles and 1 channel — ranging from Siaba Besar (5-18m) for beginner divers up to The Cauldron (Shotgun) (8-25m), advanced territory.
