Bonaire

Caribbean (Netherlands) · Caribbean — World's best shore-diving with healthy fringing reefs, dive-when-you-want freedom

Photo: Jay Brasher (CC BY 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Bonaire is at its best Apr-Jun & Oct-Dec, with Mar, Apr, May and Jun rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 26–29°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Protected leeward shore diving, minimal current. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 5 shore dives, 1 wreck, 1 reef and 1 wall — ranging from 1000 Steps (6-30m) for beginner divers up to Forest (Klein Bonaire) (12-30m), intermediate territory.
