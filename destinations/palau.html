Palau

Palau · Micronesia / Pacific — Blue Corner drift & reef-hook diving; lunar spawning aggregations.

Photo: Matt Kieffer from London, United Kingdom (CC BY-SA 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Palau is at its best Dec-Apr (Feb-Apr peak), with Feb, Mar and Apr rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 28–30°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~40m, and strong currents — Blue Corner up to 3 knots; reef hooks essential. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 3 caverns, 2 drift dives, 2 channels and 2 walls — ranging from Big Drop-Off (3-30m+) for beginner divers up to Siaes Tunnel (20-40m), advanced territory.
