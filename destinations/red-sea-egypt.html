Red Sea (Egypt) — now four destinations

We split this guide so each resort town gets its own season calendar, dive sites and photos.

  • DahabWalk-in Gulf of Aqaba diving at its most laid-back — the Blue Hole via El Bells, the Canyon's cathedral cracks, Lighthouse house-reef ease and a deep technical scene, all from the beach.
  • Hurghada & El GounaEasy warm-water reef diving out of a big dive town, with the Giftun Island coral gardens, the four-wreck graveyard of Abu Nuhas and shark-patrolled offshore pinnacles all within day-boat range.
  • Marsa AlamSouthern Red Sea base pairing easy fringing-reef shore diving and dugong seagrass bays with world-class pelagic walls — oceanic whitetips at Elphinstone from October to December, hammerheads at Daedalus and spinner dolphin pods at Sataya.
  • Sharm El SheikhAnthias-carpeted walls at Ras Mohammed, the summer spawning aggregations of snapper and barracuda off Shark and Yolanda reefs, drift dives across the four Tiran reefs and day trips to the SS Thistlegorm.
