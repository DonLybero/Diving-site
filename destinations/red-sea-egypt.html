Red Sea (Egypt)

Egypt · Red Sea / Middle East — Year-round reefs & wrecks; offshore shark reefs (Brothers/Daedalus/Elphinstone).

Photo: Derek Keats (CC BY 2.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Red Sea (Egypt) is at its best Mar-May & Sep-Nov, with Mar, Apr, May, Jun, Sep, Oct and Nov rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 22–30°C (suit: 3mm summer / 5-7mm winter), visibility up to ~30m, and medium currents — Sheltered north; strong drift at Brothers/Daedalus/Elphinstone. Divers here work 10 recognised sites — 3 walls, 2 wrecks, 2 drift dives, 2 reefs and 1 shore dive — ranging from Sha'ab Samadai (Dolphin House) (5-20m) for beginner divers up to Blue Hole (Dahab) (rim 7-30m), advanced territory.
