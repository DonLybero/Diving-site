Maui & Kona

USA · Hawaii — Kona manta-ray night dives year-round plus Molokini crater

Photo: Internet Archive Book Images (No restrictions) via Wikimedia Commons

Maui & Kona is at its best Apr-Oct, with Apr, May, Jun, Jul, Aug, Sep and Oct rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 24–27°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Calm leeward coasts; current only at exposed sites. Divers here work 10 recognised sites — 3 shore dives, 2 reefs, 2 walls, 1 manta dive, 1 cavern and 1 pinnacle — ranging from Molokini Crater (8-27m) for beginner divers up to Molokini Back Wall (10-40m+), advanced territory.
