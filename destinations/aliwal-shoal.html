Aliwal Shoal

South Africa · Indian Ocean / KwaZulu-Natal — Sardine run bait balls plus raggie and baited tiger shark dives

Photo: Andra Waagmeester (CC0) via Wikimedia Commons

Aliwal Shoal is at its best Jun-Jul (sardine run), Jun-Nov (raggies), with Jun and Jul rating as peak season. Expect subtropical water of 21–27°C (suit: 5-7mm), visibility up to ~24m, and medium currents — Moderate-to-strong drift and surge. Divers here work 9 recognised sites — 5 reefs, 2 wrecks, 1 wall and 1 shark dive — ranging from Raggie's Cave (14-16m) for beginner divers up to Aliwal Baited Shark Dive (5-15m), advanced territory.
