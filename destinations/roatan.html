Roatán

Honduras · Caribbean — Affordable wall and reef diving on the Mesoamerican Reef, great for beginners

Photo: DJ Cane (CC BY-SA 4.0) via Wikimedia Commons

Roatán is at its best Mar-Sep, with Mar, Apr, May and Jun rating as peak season. Expect tropical water of 27–30°C (suit: 3mm), visibility up to ~32m, and low currents — Calm sheltered sites, light currents. Divers here work 8 recognised sites — 2 walls, 2 wrecks, 2 channels, 1 cavern and 1 drift dive — ranging from Blue Channel (5-15m) for beginner divers up to Texas (10-30m), advanced territory.
