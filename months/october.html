Best Scuba Diving in October

Where the water is at its best in October, region by region.

← SeptemberAll monthsNovember →

October is the Red Sea's sweet spot: thinner crowds, water still holding 27°C, and oceanic-whitetip season on the offshore reefs — Brothers, Daedalus, Elphinstone. The Sea of Cortez peaks, Komodo closes its dry season strong, and the Seychelles enter a calm inter-monsoon window with whale sharks cruising off Mahé.

Africa

3 picks, all at peak season — Tofo leads.

Tofo — Mozambique

Peak 24°C 20m viz medium current

Year-round reef manta cleaning stations at Manta Reef and Giants Castle, whale sharks off the beach Oct-Mar, and migrating humpbacks Jun-Oct, all reached by RIB launches through the surf

What to expect in October: Whale sharks returning, late humpbacks, mantas. Conditions: Calm seas, warming water, clearing viz.

Full guide: Tofo →

Red Sea (Egypt) — Egypt

Peak 27°C 18m viz Medium current

Year-round reefs & wrecks; offshore shark reefs (Brothers/Daedalus/Elphinstone).

What to expect in October: Oceanic whitetip & thresher building; mantas. Conditions: Ideal warmth, calm seas.

Full guide: Red Sea (Egypt) →

Seychelles

Peak 28°C 16m viz Medium current

Granite-boulder reefs; whale shark season Aug-Nov; calm transition-month diving.

What to expect in October: Whale shark season peak (sightings rarer in recent years); mantas rising. Conditions: Transition; seas settling, viz recovering.

Full guide: Seychelles →

North America

2 picks, all at peak season — Sea of Cortez leads.

Sea of Cortez — Mexico

Peak 28°C 32m viz Medium current

Los Islotes sea lions, mobula swarms and La Paz whale sharks

What to expect in October: Sea lions, schooling fish, whale sharks. Conditions: Warm, best viz, calm seas.

Full guide: Sea of Cortez →

Maui & Kona — USA

Peak 27°C 24m viz Low current

Kona manta-ray night dives year-round plus Molokini crater

What to expect in October: Manta night dives. Conditions: Calm, clear water.

Full guide: Maui & Kona →

Asia

5 picks, all at peak season — Komodo National Park leads.

Komodo National Park — Indonesia

Peak 28°C 24m viz Strong current

Strong tidal drifts; warm north vs cold-upwelling south; mantas year-round by zone.

What to expect in October: Mola tail; N mantas (Karang Makassar); reef sharks. Conditions: Dry ends; calm; good viz.

Full guide: Komodo National Park →

Maldives

Peak 29°C 16m viz Medium current

Monsoon-driven channel drifts; Hanifaru manta aggregation Jul-Oct.

What to expect in October: Hanifaru tail; whale sharks frequent; mantas. Conditions: Mantas peak Jul-Oct; viz improving.

Full guide: Maldives →

Sipadan Island — Malaysia

Peak 28°C 50m viz Strong current

Turtle & barracuda-tornado wall; island closed every November.

What to expect in October: Barracuda tornado, turtles, reef sharks. Conditions: 2nd excellent window; viz 30-50m+.

Full guide: Sipadan Island →

Okinawa Islands — Japan

Peak 27°C 32m viz Strong current

Winter hammerhead schools at Yonaguni, summer mantas at Ishigaki

What to expect in October: Mantas, turtles. Conditions: Warm, excellent viz.

Full guide: Okinawa Islands →

Cenderawasih Bay — Indonesia

Peak 28°C 25m viz Low current

The only place on Earth to scuba year-round with resident whale sharks feeding under fishermen's bagan platforms, plus coral-clad WWII wrecks in glassy, currentless water.

What to expect in October: Whale sharks year-round; healthy reefs and coral-clad wrecks. Conditions: End of peak season; still calm and clear before the wetter months.

Full guide: Cenderawasih Bay →

South America

2 picks, all at peak season — Galapagos Islands leads.

Galapagos Islands — Ecuador

Peak 21°C 10m viz Strong current

Big-animal mecca; whale sharks & hammerhead schools at Darwin/Wolf.

What to expect in October: Whale sharks tapering (still peak); hammerheads; mola. Conditions: Cool; rough; plankton-green.

Full guide: Galapagos Islands →

Fernando de Noronha — Brazil

Peak 27°C 32m viz Medium current

Huge spinner dolphin pods with sharks, turtles and 30m+ visibility

What to expect in October: Spinner dolphins, lemon sharks, turtles. Conditions: Dry, superb viz, calm.

Full guide: Fernando de Noronha →

French Polynesia

Central America

Cocos Island carries the region this month.

Cocos Island — Costa Rica

Peak 25°C 10m viz Strong current

Hammerhead-school mecca; remote 36h crossing, strong currents/thermoclines.

What to expect in October: Hammerhead/whale-shark peak; mantas. Conditions: Wet peak; rough crossings; green water.
