Best Scuba Diving in April

Where the water is at its best in April, region by region.

← MarchAll monthsMay →

April is transition month done right. Palau closes its peak with lunar spawning aggregations, Tubbataha's short liveaboard season opens in the Sulu Sea, Komodo warms into manta season and Malapascua's threshers keep showing. The Caribbean stays calm and clear — and Utila's whale-shark run begins.

Asia

5 picks, all at peak season — Layang Layang leads.

Layang Layang — Malaysia

Peak 29°C 40m viz Strong current

A remote oceanic atoll where walls plunge 2,000 m and springtime brings schooling scalloped hammerheads in gin-clear water.

What to expect in April: Hammerhead aggregations at their best, mantas and grey reef sharks. Conditions: Calmest month; superb visibility.

Full guide: Layang Layang →

Fuvahmulah — Maldives

Peak 29°C 33m viz Strong current

The tiger shark capital of the world, where a shallow harbour plateau puts you eye-to-eye with resident tigers while deep drop-offs deliver threshers, oceanic mantas and schooling hammerheads.

What to expect in April: Tigers year-round; oceanic mantas building toward their mating peak, with whale sharks and lingering hammerheads.. Conditions: Tail of the dry northeast monsoon, still calm before the seasonal change..

Full guide: Fuvahmulah →

Malapascua Island — Philippines

Peak 29°C 32m viz Low current

Year-round dawn thresher sharks at Kimud Shoal (formerly Monad, now visited by tiger sharks), plus rich macro

What to expect in April: Threshers, mantas, devil rays. Conditions: Flat seas, excellent visibility.

Full guide: Malapascua Island →

Komodo National Park — Indonesia

Peak 28°C 18m viz Strong current

Strong tidal drifts; warm north vs cold-upwelling south; mantas year-round by zone.

What to expect in April: N/Central mantas active; whale sharks (Apr-Jun); reef sharks. Conditions: Dry begins; SWEET SPOT; manageable currents.

Full guide: Komodo National Park →

Similan Islands — Thailand

Peak 30°C 18m viz Medium current

Granite boulders, mantas and whale sharks in the Andaman Sea, liveaboard season only

What to expect in April: Mantas, whale sharks, leopard sharks. Conditions: Warm, calm, top visibility.

Full guide: Similan Islands →

Oceania

3 picks, all at peak season — Palau leads.

Palau

Peak 29°C 40m viz Strong current

Blue Corner drift & reef-hook diving; lunar spawning aggregations.

What to expect in April: Grey-reef shark peak; bumphead spawning; mantas; whale sharks. Conditions: Best month; calm; viz 30-40m+.

Full guide: Palau →

Ningaloo Reef — Australia

Peak 27°C 18m viz Low current

Whale shark snorkeling on the reef plus year-round mantas and turtles

What to expect in April: Whale sharks, mantas, turtles. Conditions: Great viz, calm, warm.

Full guide: Ningaloo Reef →

Yap — Micronesia (FSM)

Peak 29°C 28m viz Medium current

Resident reef mantas queue at channel cleaning stations year-round, peaking in the Dec-Apr mating season, with dusk mandarinfish and shark-lined caverns on the side.

What to expect in April: Tail end of the manta mating peak; cleaning stations remain busy.. Conditions: Dry-to-wet transition begins; mostly calm with the first passing showers..

Full guide: Yap →

Central America

Coiba carries the region this month.

Coiba — Panama

Peak 28°C 20m viz strong current

A Pacific UNESCO marine park where cool, nutrient-rich upwelling feeds schooling scalloped hammerheads (best March-July), resident whitetip reef sharks, seasonal whale sharks and mantas, plus walls of jacks and barracuda over coral-clad seamounts.

What to expect in April: Prime hammerhead season with the biggest schools, plus whitetips, mantas and mobula rays. Conditions: End of dry season, cool nutrient-rich currents, variable viz on thermoclines.

Full guide: Coiba →

Caribbean

2 picks, all at peak season — Utila leads.

Utila — Honduras

Peak 28°C 32m viz Low current

Budget dive-cert hub with year-round whale shark chances

What to expect in April: Whale sharks, turtles, seahorses. Conditions: Whale shark season, flat seas, top viz.

Full guide: Utila →

Great Blue Hole — Belize

Peak 28°C 30m viz Low current

Iconic deep blue sinkhole with stalactites and circling reef sharks

What to expect in April: Reef sharks; Gladden Spit whale sharks. Conditions: Dry season peak, calm, viz 30m+.

Full guide: Great Blue Hole →

Africa

2 picks, all at peak season — Red Sea (Egypt) leads.

Red Sea (Egypt) — Egypt

Peak 24°C 18m viz Medium current

Year-round reefs & wrecks; offshore shark reefs (Brothers/Daedalus/Elphinstone).

What to expect in April: Big Fish season; mantas, early whale sharks. Conditions: Warm, calm; ideal shoulder.

Full guide: Red Sea (Egypt) →

Seychelles

Peak 30°C 30m viz Medium current

Granite-boulder reefs; whale shark season Aug-Nov; calm transition-month diving.

What to expect in April: Whale sharks (Amirantes Apr-May peak). Conditions: Transition: flat seas, best viz 25-35m, 30C.

Full guide: Seychelles →

North America

Socorro Island — Mexico

Peak 24°C 32m viz Strong current

Liveaboard-only Revillagigedo with friendly giant oceanic mantas

What to expect in April: Giant mantas, dolphins. Conditions: Warming, excellent viz.

Maui & Kona — USA

Peak 25°C 24m viz Low current

Kona manta-ray night dives year-round plus Molokini crater

What to expect in April: Manta night dives, turtles. Conditions: Calm, clear water.
