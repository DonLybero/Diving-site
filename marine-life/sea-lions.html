Sea Lions

The puppies of the sea — curious, fast, and full of mischief.

Diving with sea lions is pure play. Young ones barrel-roll around your fins, mouth your camera dome, blow bubbles in your face and dare you to keep up — all speed and curiosity. They haul out on rocky islets and colonies, and the bolder juveniles turn any dive into a game. It is the most joyful encounter in the ocean, and it asks for nothing but patience and a willingness to play along.

Good to know
  • Let them come to you and mirror their play — sea lions lose interest in stiff, staring divers.
  • Give the big bulls a wide berth, especially in breeding season when they guard their patch.
  • Most colonies are cool-water, so a 5-7 mm suit keeps the fun going longer.
