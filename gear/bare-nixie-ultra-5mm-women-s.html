Bare Nixie Ultra 5mm Women's Review & Best Price

A women's-specific 5mm with graphene warmth cut for a true female fit.

#7 in Top Wetsuits in 2026

What makes it good?

BARE builds the Nixie Ultra around two things: a women-specific pattern and the OMNIRED lining, which weaves 13 thermo-reactive minerals into the interior fabric to reflect body heat back as infrared. In the water that reads as a 5 mm that runs warmer than its thickness, and divers who move between plain-lined suits and this one consistently notice the difference on longer dives. The size run is the other real asset: 2 through 14 with tall and plus variants of most sizes, cut by a women's design team rather than scaled down from a men's block. Donning is easier than most 5 mm suits thanks to zippers at the collar, wrists and ankles, and the Glideskin collar seals without the raw-neck feel of a stitched edge. Full-stretch neoprene throughout keeps shoulder mobility close to a 3 mm suit. The price is the catch — around $430 puts it at the top of the 5 mm band — and the tall and plus sizes sell through at US retailers. Seams carry a 7-year warranty against the 2-year cover on the rest, which says something about where BARE expects it to fail — nowhere soon.

Where it wins

  • The OMNIRED interior weaves 13 thermo-reactive minerals into the lining to reflect body heat back as infrared — divers rate it noticeably warmer than plain-lined 5 mm suits
  • Zippers at the collar, wrists and ankles cut the usual 5 mm donning wrestle to a minimum
  • Patterned by BARE's women's design team with tall and plus variants of most sizes (04T, 08+, 12+), so long torsos and wider hips get a real cut, not a scaled men's suit
  • The Glideskin collar seals against flushing without chafing the neck
  • Seams are warrantied for 7 years, well past the 2-year cover on the rest of the suit

Where it falls short

  • At around $430 it sits at the top of the 5 mm price band
  • The tall and plus sizes drift in and out of stock at US retailers, so the fit advantage can mean waiting
  • One colorway — black — with no lighter option for divers who overheat topside in the sun
