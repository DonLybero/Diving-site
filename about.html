About DiveSZN

DiveSZN is a seasonal dive-trip planner. It scores world dive destinations month by month so you can match your travel dates to the places where the season, the water and the wildlife line up — and it pairs that with independent scuba-gear buyer’s guides so you arrive with the right kit.

How the data is made

The seasonal calendar covers every destination on the site across all twelve months — water temperature, visibility, currents, marine-life timing and a season rating for each. It is compiled and cross-checked against dive-operator and liveaboard calendars, marine-park authorities and ocean sea-temperature sources, then hand-verified. Water temperatures are typical monthly ranges (±1°C) and marine-life timing shifts year to year with plankton and lunar cycles, so we always say the same thing: confirm current conditions with a local dive centre before you travel.

Beyond the planner

The same seasonal data powers our marine-life guides — where and when to dive whale sharks, mantas, hammerheads and more — and the month-by-month guides that rank where diving is at its best for any travel date.

Our gear guides

Gear picks are researched independently and chosen on the merits. Prices shown are indicative as of our research date — the retailer always shows the live price. DiveSZN is reader-supported: some “Buy” links are affiliate links and we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Commissions never influence our rankings.

Scuba only

DiveSZN is written for scuba divers. Every recommendation, from a destination’s best months to a wetsuit’s thickness, is framed around scuba diving.
