# Rename: Scubanaut → DiveSZN (2026-07)

The site was renamed **DiveSZN** ("dive season", sports-slang "SZN" spelling)
in 2026-07. The name states the product's USP — seasonal, month-by-month dive
planning — and was chosen from a 13-agent naming study that screened ~60
candidates across eight creative lanes (explorer, seasonal, lyrical, coined,
scuba-explicit, hype, luxury, adventure, creature).

**This is research, not legal advice** — a certified USPTO/EUIPO knockout
search by a trademark attorney is still the OWNER action before merch or paid
marketing.

## Why the old name was dropped

"Scubanaut" screened MEDIUM risk: crowded common-law field (SCUBAnauts
International nonprofit + Florida state mark, FOLIOT "Scubanaut" dive watches,
dive centres in MY/PH/IN), and every good domain taken. Full record:
`docs/trademark-search-2026-07.md`.

## DiveSZN screen results (2026-07, indexed mirrors + DNS — not certified)

- **Conflicts:** none found. No company, app, dive brand, music artist, or
  crypto project named "DiveSZN"/"Dive SZN" surfaced in web-wide search.
- **Trademarks:** no "DIVE SZN" filing found on indexed USPTO mirrors
  (Justia/Trademarkia). Only compound-SZN mark found: "RAWR SZN"
  (entertainment, TX) — unrelated mark and class.
- **Domains at screen time:** diveszn.com, diveszn.io, diveszn.app all
  unresolved in DNS (likely unregistered) — the only full trifecta among all
  ~60 names screened.
- **Risk rating:** LOW.
- Caveats: "SZN" reads letter-by-letter to audiences outside sports/streetwear
  culture; screen methods were web-index + DNS because USPTO/RDAP were not
  directly reachable from the research environment.
